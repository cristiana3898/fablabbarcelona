Arduino

    Arduino is an open source computer hardware, composed by electronic board equipped with sets of digital and analog input/output, microcontroller, pins that may be interact with expansion boards (shields) and other circuits, a reset button, and some of them can have Wi-fi.
    The boards feature serial communications interfaces, also USB on some models, which are also used for loading programs from computers.
