The vinyl cutter

The vinyl cutter is, as the name, is a type of computer-controlled machine (small vinyl cutters look like computer printers) where the computer controls the movement of a sharp blade. This blade is used to cut out shapes and letters from sheets of thin self-adhesive plastic (vinyl). The vinyl can be stuck onto almost any surface.

The machine works with vector image and it cuts along the vector paths laid out in the design. The cutter is capable of moving the blade on an X and Y axis over the material, cutting it into any shape imaginable.

  • Check for imperfections (scale it) and if is necessary
