Scan with a Kinect

    This is a simple way to create a 3d object. You can also scan yourself and create a mini-you!
    You have to connect the kinect to the pc and download the Skanect app. When it is open click on NEW and select what you want to scan and the size of the visual, then click on START.
    Now you have to go to RECORD and start.
    After the scansion yuo can go in PROCESS and remove the parts that you don't want and fill the holes.
    Then save it in .stl or .obj
    (if you have also some holes you can use MESHMIXER where you can SELECT the holes then press X and go to ANALYSIS and then INSPECTOR and let it to auto-repair all)
    The last thing to do is print it!
