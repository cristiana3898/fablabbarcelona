    • Bio Plastic fabric

  • To create a Bio plastic fabric to produce an apron you have to take the recipe and the ingredients and some containers.
  • The ingredients:
  • -250ml of water,
  • -50g of gelatine,
  • -20g of glicerina vegetal.
    • We put the water in the pot on the stove, then we started add the gelatine while mixing it, we have to be careful to don't let form lumps in the liquid. We let it boil and then we put the glicerina. Then the gelatine started create a white foam that we remove with a spoon. Then we add the acrylic paint and mix it all over gain. After taking away the foam we inverted the liquid on a table and let it dry for some days.
